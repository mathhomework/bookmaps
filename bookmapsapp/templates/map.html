About BookMaps

BookMaps is a location-based book discovery tool for discovering your next read. It works by mapping out books based on the setting in which they took place. Navigate around the map, and books will start popping up!

You can also add your favorite book to BookMap's community database by using the "Add a Book" function.

This is an ongoing project by Andrew Shum. Please send questions or comments to: andrewshum@alumni.ucsd.edu
